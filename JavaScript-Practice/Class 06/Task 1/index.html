Next-gen bioprinting:

In 2025, advancements in 3D bioprinting using bio-inks containing stem cells allow for the precise creation of functional, human-like tissues. AI is being used to optimize tissue design in real-time.


Organoids for drug trials:

Researchers are growing organoids, or "mini-organs," from a patient's stem cells to test drug effectiveness and safety before human trials.


iPSC advancements:

Techniques for creating iPSCs are becoming safer and more efficient. Non-viral methods like mRNA transfection and small molecules are replacing older viral methods, reducing the risk of genetic alteration.

Artificial Intelligence (AI)

Artificial Intelligence refers to systems that perform tasks which normally require human intelligence. These systems analyze data, recognize patterns, and make decisions or recommendations. Common examples include virtual assistants, image and speech recognition, and recommendation engines that personalize content or services.

Blockchain

Blockchain is a decentralized ledger technology that records transactions in a secure, tamper-resistant way. Each record (or block) links to the previous one, creating a chain that is distributed across many participants. This design supports trustless transactions, transparent audit trails, and applications like secure record-keeping, tokenized assets, and smart contracts.

Quantum Computing

Quantum computing uses quantum bits (qubits) that can represent multiple states at once, enabling certain computations to be done differently than on classical computers. Quantum machines are explored for tasks such as optimization, cryptography research, and simulating complex physical systems where classical approaches may be slow or impractical.
